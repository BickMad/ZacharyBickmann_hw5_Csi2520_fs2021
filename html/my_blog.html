Check out this New York Times Article, Rivian will start trading today after an I.P.O valuing it at nearly $70 billion

Rivan is a new electric car company that has plans to start delivering its first all electric truck to customers by the end of 2021. This automaker is backed by both Amazon and Ford. The crazy thing is that despite having almost no sales, the companys initial public offering is going to value the company at almost $70 billion dollars. Ford which is valued at $80 billion dollars sold more than four million vehicles last year.
